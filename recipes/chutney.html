Chutney

Chutney is a flavorful condiment from Indian cuisine, made by grinding or blending herbs, spices, fruits, or vegetables into a coarse or smooth paste. It balances tanginess, spice, and freshness, often used to enhance the taste of main dishes. Whether coconut-based, minty, tangy with tamarind, or spicy with chilies, chutneys add a burst of flavor to every bite. They are commonly served alongside dosa, idli, rice, chapati, or fried snacks.

Ingredients :

  • Grated fresh coconut
  • Roasted chana dal
  • Green chillies
  • Ginger piece
  • Coconut oil
  • Curry leaves
  • Dried red chillies

Prepare Chutney

  1. Grind Chutney: Blend coconut, chana dal, chillies, ginger, sal and add a little water
  2. Heat coconut oil in a small pan, Add mustard seeds
  3. Pour the tempering over the ground chutney. Mix well and serve.

Now, serve it with Hot Idly or Dosa :))
