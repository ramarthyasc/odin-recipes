Fish Curry

Kerala Fish Curry is a traditional South Indian dish known for its bold flavors and rich, tangy gravy. Made with fresh fish simmered in a blend of spices, tamarind (or kokum), curry leaves, and often coconut or coconut milk, it captures the essence of Keralaâ€™s coastal cuisine.

Typically served with steamed rice or kappa (tapioca), Kerala fish curry is a staple in Malayali homes and offers a perfect balance of heat, acidity, and deep coastal flavor.

Ingredients :

  • Fresh fish
  • Coconut oil
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chillies
  • Onion
  • Turmeric powder

Prepare Fish Curry

  1. Prepare the masala Paste
  2. Tempering
  3. Add onions/shallors
  4. Add spice paste
  5. Add tamarind water
  6. Add fish
  7. Rest the curry

Now serve hot with steamed rice, or tapioca :)
