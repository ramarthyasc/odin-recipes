Sambar

Sambar is a traditional South Indian lentil stew made with toor dal, mixed vegetables, tamarind, and a unique blend of spices and herbs. It's known for its perfect balance of tangy, spicy, and savory flavors. The use of sambar powder, curry leaves, mustard seeds, and hing (asafoetida) gives it a rich and aromatic profile.

Ingredients :

  • Toor dal
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Tomato
  • Tamarind
  • Turmeric
  • Sambar powder
  • Salt
  • Red Chilli
  • Curry leaves

Prepare Sambar

  1. Cook dal
  2. Prepare Vegetables
  3. Add Tamarind & Spices
  4. Prepare Tampering

Now, serve Sambar with steamed rice :))
